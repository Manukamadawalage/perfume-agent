# Default Fragrance Ratios for Seasons/Occasions

## Summer Fragrance
- **Top Notes:** 40% (citrus, light florals, green notes)
- **Middle Notes:** 35% (floral, green, aquatic)
- **Base Notes:** 25% (woody, amber, light musks)

## Winter Fragrance
- **Top Notes:** 25% (spicy, woody)
- **Middle Notes:** 40% (oriental, floral, spices)
- **Base Notes:** 35% (amber, balsamic, resinous)

## Daily Wear (Everyday Fragrance)
- **Top Notes:** 30% (citrus, herbal)
- **Middle Notes:** 40% (floral, green)
- **Base Notes:** 30% (woody, light musk)

## Special Occasions (e.g., Formal, Evening)
- **Top Notes:** 25% (spicy, citrus, floral)
- **Middle Notes:** 50% (floral, warm spicy, sweet)
- **Base Notes:** 25% (balsamic, woody, musky)

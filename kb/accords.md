# Fragrance Accords

## Floral Accord
- **Top Notes:** Rose, Jasmine, Lavender
- **Middle Notes:** Ylang Ylang, Geranium, Violet
- **Base Notes:** Musk, Sandalwood

## Woody Accord
- **Top Notes:** Cedarwood, Pine, Fir
- **Middle Notes:** Patchouli, Vetiver
- **Base Notes:** Sandalwood, Amber, Oud

## Gourmand Accord
- **Top Notes:** Almond, Lemon, Orange
- **Middle Notes:** Chocolate, Caramel
- **Base Notes:** Vanilla, Tonka Bean, Musk

## Spicy Accord
- **Top Notes:** Black Pepper, Ginger, Cardamom
- **Middle Notes:** Cinnamon, Clove, Nutmeg
- **Base Notes:** Patchouli, Cedarwood, Musk

## Citrus Accord
- **Top Notes:** Lemon, Orange, Bergamot
- **Middle Notes:** Neroli, Petitgrain
- **Base Notes:** Amber, Musk
